About

About Carbon Consulting.

An IT advisory and consulting firm working with leadership teams on technology decisions and projects.

What we do

An advisory practice.

Carbon Consulting is an IT advisory and consulting firm. We help organizations plan their technology strategy, select software and vendors, oversee implementations, and clean up IT operations and spend.

Who we serve

Leaders making consequential decisions.

Our clients are mid-market and enterprise organizations — typically $25M to $2B in revenue — across financial services, healthcare, professional services, manufacturing, nonprofit, and education. Most engagements are initiated by CIOs, CTOs, CFOs, COOs, and boards.

What our clients have in common is that the decision or project at hand matters — operationally, financially, or both — and they want it handled by people who have done this work before.
