About

About Carbon Consulting.

An independent IT advisory and consulting firm working with leadership teams on the decisions and projects that shape their technology environment.

What we do

A focused advisory practice.

Carbon Consulting is an IT advisory and consulting firm. We help organizations plan their technology strategy, select software and vendors, oversee implementations, and clean up IT operations and spend.

We're not a managed service provider, we don't resell software, and we don't have vendor partnerships. Our clients hire us for clear-eyed advice and for project work that gets done on time and within scope.

Who we serve

Leaders making consequential decisions.

Our clients are mid-market and enterprise organizations — typically $25M to $2B in revenue — across financial services, healthcare, professional services, manufacturing, nonprofit, and education. Most engagements are initiated by CIOs, CTOs, CFOs, COOs, and boards.

What our clients have in common is that the decision or project at hand matters — operationally, financially, or both — and they want it handled by senior people who've done it before.

How we work

Three commitments.

01

Senior Expertise

Every engagement is led by a senior consultant from start to finish. When you meet someone in the initial conversation, that's the person who will do the work.

02

Independent Advice

We're an advisory firm, not a reseller or managed services provider. Our only revenue is the fee our clients pay us for the engagement.

03

Clear Outcomes

Every engagement has a written scope, a fixed or not-to-exceed fee, and specific deliverables. The engagement ends when the scope is done.
