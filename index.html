FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What does Carbon Consulting do?

We're an IT advisory and consulting firm. We help organizations plan their technology strategy, select the right software and vendors, manage implementations, and clean up IT operations. We don't sell software or managed services — our only product is advice and project delivery.

How is this different from a managed IT provider?

A managed IT provider runs your day-to-day IT operations. We're an advisory firm — we help you make decisions and run projects. Many of our clients already have an IT team or MSP in place; we work alongside them on the bigger strategic and project work.

How do you charge?

Every engagement has a written proposal with a fixed fee or a not-to-exceed fee. We don't bill open-ended hourly, and we don't extend scope to extend an engagement.

How long does an engagement take?

Most engagements run 6 to 16 weeks, depending on scope. Implementation oversight engagements can run longer — 3 to 12 months — with a regular cadence.

Do you work remotely or on-site?

Both. Most work is done remotely, with on-site presence for kickoffs, key workshops, and executive presentations.

Who will I actually work with?

The senior consultant you meet in the initial conversation is the one who will lead and deliver your engagement. We don't hand engagements off after the sales process.
