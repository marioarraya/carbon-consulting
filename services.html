Services

Our services.

Five focused services. Used individually or together. Every engagement is scoped and priced in writing before work begins.

01 IT Strategy & Roadmap 02 Software & Vendor Selection 03 Implementation Oversight 04 IT Governance 05 Vendor Consolidation
01 Roadmap
No. 01

IT Strategy & Roadmap

A clear technology plan that matches your business goals, budget, and timeline.

What's included
  • Review of current systems, infrastructure, and IT spend
  • Assessment of gaps, risks, and opportunities
  • Multi-year roadmap with priorities and sequencing
  • Cost estimates and budget planning
  • Executive and board-ready presentation
Best for
Companies planning a major IT investment, preparing for growth, or bringing structure to reactive IT spending.
Typical duration
6–10 weeks
02 Harmony
No. 02

Software & Vendor Selection

A structured process for choosing the right software, platform, or service provider.

What's included
  • Requirements gathering from end users and leadership
  • Market research and shortlist development (3–5 vendors)
  • Structured RFP or demo process with scoring
  • Reference checks and due diligence
  • Commercial analysis and contract negotiation support
  • Written recommendation with scored comparison
Common use cases
ERP, CRM, EHR, financial systems, HR platforms, managed service providers, core line-of-business applications.
Typical duration
8–14 weeks
03 Oversight
No. 03

Implementation Oversight

Independent project management for technology implementations.

What's included
  • Project plan, governance structure, and meeting cadence
  • Milestone tracking and deliverable reviews
  • Risk identification and issue escalation
  • Vendor performance management
  • Executive status reporting
  • Intervention when projects go off track
Engagement models
Lead — Carbon runs the project end to end. Oversight — Carbon provides independent oversight alongside your vendor or internal team.
Typical duration
3–12 months
04 Governance
No. 04

IT Governance

Clean up roles, responsibilities, documentation, and controls across your IT function.

What's included
  • Full inventory of systems, vendors, and contracts
  • Roles and responsibilities (RACI) matrix
  • Key process documentation
  • IT policy framework aligned to your industry
  • Plan for ongoing ownership by your internal team
Best for
Organizations preparing for an audit, coming out of rapid growth, post-acquisition integration, or building IT governance for the first time.
Typical duration
4–8 weeks
05 Consolidation
No. 05

Vendor Consolidation

Reduce overlap, renegotiate contracts, and lower your total IT spend.

What's included
  • Complete inventory of IT vendors, software, and subscriptions
  • Analysis of overlap, underuse, and auto-renewals
  • Consolidation plan with projected savings
  • Contract renegotiation on material vendors
  • Execution of the consolidation
Typical savings
15–35% of annual IT vendor spend
Typical duration
6–12 weeks (analysis); implementation varies
How We Engage

How engagements work.

  1. 01

    Start with a conversation.

    Most engagements begin with a 30-minute call. We'll understand your situation, answer questions, and tell you honestly whether we're the right firm for the work.

  2. 02

    Proposal in writing.

    If there's a fit, you'll get a written proposal with scope, deliverables, timeline, and a fixed or not-to-exceed fee. Nothing starts until it's signed.

  3. 03

    Deliver and transfer.

    We deliver the work, document it, and transfer everything to your team at the end. No ongoing dependency unless you specifically want one.
